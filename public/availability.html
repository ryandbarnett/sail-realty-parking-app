Parking Availability

  • No public parking Monday–Friday, 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM (reserved for Sail Realty staff).
  • Public parking is available evenings, weekends, and recognized holidays.
  • Overnight parking is allowed, but maximum stay is 24 hours.

Recognized Holidays

Public parking is allowed all day on the following holidays. Standard rates and the 24-hour maximum still apply.

  • New Year’s Day (Jan 1)
  • Memorial Day
  • Independence Day (Jul 4)
  • Labor Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Day after Thanksgiving
  • Holiday Range: December 24 (Christmas Eve) through January 1 (New Year’s Day)
